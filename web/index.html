What's happening behind the scenes?

When you upload a photo and tap Analyze, the picture is sent to a small neural network that has already studied hundreds of fresh and rotten tomatoes. It takes a careful look and answers with its best guess — fresh or rotten — along with how sure it is.

The dataset source, model layers, training results, and local setup steps are documented in the public project README.
